F.A.Q

  • How do I change the Settings?
    • Tap on the Menu Bar on the top Right and select the Settings Menu Item. Once in the Settings Menu, you can change the desired Settings.
  • How do I save a conversion?
    • Tap the Save Conversion Button and then enter a valid save name.
  • How do I load a save?
    • Tap on the Menu Bar on the top Right and select the Saved Conversions Menu Item. Once in the Saved Conversions Activity, select the desired save and click Load Save.
  • How do I delete a save?
    • Tap on the Menu Bar on the top Right and select the Saved Conversions Menu Item. Once in the Saved Conversions Activity, select the desired save and click Delete Save.
  • How do I perform a historical conversion?
    • Tap the Historical Conversion Button and then enter a valid date.
  • How do I refresh the conversion data?
    • Tap the Refesh Button and Tap OK if you want to use an API call.
